Hunting LM Property

Requirements

  • Member of SHFC in good standing
  • Complete 5 hours of workbond for the club.
  • Take and pass a bowhunter education class and have the card
  • Must attend one of the scheduled mandatory bowhunting safety briefing. (usually held weekly in July/August).
  • A valid deer or elk license (or fall turkey license) that includes unit 461.
  • Once all requirements are met, the applicant will interface getting a permit from LM plant protection who will verify all requirements are met and issue a hunting permission permit. This is thru the Safety briefing only

Noteworthy:

  • You can hunt with either a rifle or archery tag using a bow only, however if bowhunting with a rifle tag, the 500 sq/in hunter orange requirement is still in effect (per CP&W regulations)
  • The type of tags are:
      General archery deer or elk
      General rifle (1st-4th seasons) deer/elk
      PLO (private land only rifle tags) deer/elk
      Fall Turkey (rifle)
  • No muzzle loader big game tags allowed since a bow is not a legal weapon for Muzzleloader season(per CP&W regulations)

Bow Hunter ED

You will need Bow Hunter ED (not just Hunter ED). See the flyer for the bowhunter classes that will be held for Colorado. Please contact Colorado parks and wildlife to get scheduled for class (limited numbers per class). Also see the screen shot of the rules for archery hunting on LM property.

Archery Hunting Briefing

Five Archery Hunting Briefing (2 hr.each) days are planned in late JUNE and JULY. Attendance in one of these briefings is required to receive special archery hunting permission permit from security for hunting on LMCO property. Please do not call security for these permits. The LM property is a draw area (461). Contact James Q Gilmore for any question.
